Case Study

Project Chimera

Our flagship achievement is 'Project Chimera,' an AI model that successfully synthesized novel protein structures for therapeutic use, reducing the typical discovery timeline from years to weeks. This breakthrough, achieved by Megsy's unique approach to pattern recognition in biological data, was recently published in the Journal of Computational Biology.
